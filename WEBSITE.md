# Winteriors Website Improvement Plan
## Cover

## Introduction
This document accompanies a job proposal for the role of Online Sales Associate at Winteriors. As part of that proposal, I have prepared a live demo of an updated Winteriors website, available at [winteriors.kwamwena.link](https://winteriors.kwamwena.link). This document is a record of everything I found wrong with the current website and what I did to address it.

The Winteriors website is the digital face of the brand. It is the first point of contact for many potential customers and, as such, it needs to be in good shape. Going through the website, it became clear that there were a number of issues that could leave visitors with a poor impression of the business. These range from broken links and missing content to placeholder text and misplace images.

Each issue is described clearly, alongside what the expected behaviour should be and the solution I applied. The goal is not only to have a working website, but to ensure that anyone visiting Winteriors online gets an experience that reflects the quality of the brand. It is also important that the Winteriors team understands what was done and why, so that they can follow along and ask questions where needed

## Issue: The header is not visible.
The apparent header or top bar of the website appears to be okay from initial view. However, it is clear from inspection that this is a top bar meant to complement a header. The header is absent from view. 
![Issue: Absent Header](issues/issue-missing-header.png)

### Expected Behaviour
The header should be present. It is the section of the website which showcases the title of the website as well as the links to other parts of the website. Users expect to see this when they visit a website.

### Suggested Solution
I have added a header which includes the company name, "Winteriors" and the links to the main sections of the website. These are the Shop section, About us section, and Contact us section. Plus a menu button which shows the links when viewing the website using a phone.
![Fix: Absent Header](issues/fix-missing-header.png)
